Cesana Jewels

A simple brand identity

The logo

When Filippo first started his business, he was searching for a logo that could become recognizable, easily memorable, and that could grow alongside his business.

The result had to be something simple that could represent his brand: Cesana Jewels. From the very beginning, the initials stood out; all that was needed was a graphic form that could express professionalism and preciousness, while maintaining a modern and captivating approach.

After several comparisons and studies of various drafts, we chose the only solution that incorporated all the necessary characteristics.

Merchandise and promotional items

Subsequently, we worked on the materials required for use on social media, product packaging, and to best convey his brand image.

I provided the graphics for the labels, both in large format for advertising and public distribution, and for application to the packaging.

Additionally, Filippo was provided with all the materials necessary to maintain a consistent and cohesive image for his social media profile, but above all, I designed an animation to be used in his commercials and videos.

The video was created using 3D modeling and animation in Blender. The final result was rendered in 8K to be used in the commercials, event at cinematic quality.
