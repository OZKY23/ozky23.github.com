About me


My creative journey began with a deep fascination for animation and interaction. For five years, I delved into the world of video post-production, mastering tools like Adobe Creative Suite, Blender 3D, and more. This experience ignited a passion for crafting engaging, visually compelling narratives.

Seeking to further amplify the impact of these stories, I embarked on a five-year exploration of web development. Mastering languages like TypeScript, Angular, HTML5, CSS, and Sass, I discovered a powerful extension of my skillset. Web development became a platform to bring those narratives to life, not just on screen, but in an interactive, user-driven experience.

Throughout my career, I've collaborated with a wide range of private clients and entrepreneurs. From websites and social media content to visual branding and video productions, I've applied my understanding of informatics, communication, and visual design (including UX principles, color theory, and human interaction) to create not just functional, but truly engaging experiences.
